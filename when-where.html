When & Where

Wedding Ceremony

Saturday, February 26, 2022, 11.00 AM - 12.00 PM @ Jesser Point Beach

Join us for the exchanges of vows on the lovely Jesser Piont Beach. The Ceremony will be on the beach so please wear sunscreen, hats and sunglasses. Come with flip-Flops or even barefoot. Casual and Cool clothes is a must. A tractor will shuttle everybody down to the beach from Mseni 30min before the ceremony begins.

We will be supplying some hats and sunglasses if you do forget to bring you own.

Wedding Party

Saturday, February 26, 2022, 12.00 PM - 09.30 PM @ Mseni Beach Lodge

Please join use for an epic celebration party at Mseni Beach Lodge. Social Hour will be held from 12.00 PM till the newly wed's return from the photoshoot.

We will be seated at the venue from 02.00 PM and the main meal will be served at 03.00 PM.

There will be a cash bar and a party never to be forgotten.
