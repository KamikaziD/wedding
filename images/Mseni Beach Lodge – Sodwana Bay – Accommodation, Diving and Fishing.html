Mseni Beach Lodge – A Place of Happiness…

Situated in the heart of Sodwana Bay, an UNESCO World Heritage Site, you will find Mseni Beach Lodge – the place of happiness, where laughter and joy resounds!
Here at Mseni, we aim to provide friendly service & an excellent experience to compliment our awesome ocean views!
Our comfortably modern cottages and lovely beach house, are nestled among beautiful indigenous bush, which forms part of the iSimangaliso nature reserve.
